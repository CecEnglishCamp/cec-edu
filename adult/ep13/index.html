📖 Story Preview

Young-ja feels sick. She goes to the pharmacy. She says, “My nose is water. My throat is fire.” The pharmacist says, “Dry cough or wet cough?” Young-ja says, “Maybe… medium?” The pharmacist laughs and gives her two medicines. She asks, “Take now or tomorrow?” He says, “Now is okay.” She smiles and says, “I’m cold but happy!”...
