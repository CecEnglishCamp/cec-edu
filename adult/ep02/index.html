🏠 Daughter's Apartment

딸의 아파트에서 — Apartment Living

⭐ Key Expressions

✓Sorry, the house is a little messy.
미안해요, 집이 좀 어질러져 있어요.

📖 Story Preview

Young-ja visits her daughter. Her daughter smiles. She says, "Sorry, Mom. The house is messy." Young-ja looks around. There is pizza on the table. There are clothes on the sofa. She sees something sleeping — maybe a cat? Young-ja says, "It's okay. I lived like this in 1987." Her daughter says, "Really?" Young-ja smiles. "No. Just kidding." They drink tea together and smile. 🍵...

💬 Dialog

"엄마! 오랜만이에요. 그런데… 조금 지저분할 수도 있어요."

"괜찮아. 엄마도 예전에 이보다 더한 데서 살아봤어. 1987년에 말이지."

"정말요?"

"아니. 그냥 위로해주려고." 😄
