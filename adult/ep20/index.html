👩‍🍳 Cooking at Home

집에서 요리 — Cooking English

⭐ Key Expressions

✓I'd like to return this.
이거 반품하고 싶어요.

📖 Story Preview

Young-ja wants to return shoes. She prints a label. She puts tape on the label. The man says, “I can’t see the barcode!” Young-ja says, “I protect it!” Later, she gets an email. “No return. No label.” Young-ja is sad....

💬 Dialog

“엄마, 마음에 안 들면 온라인 쇼핑은 그냥 반품하면 돼요.”

“Return? Refund? Exchange? 뭐가 뭔지…”

“Here! I’m return!”

“Uh… there’s no barcode visible.”

“There is! But I protect it! With tape!”

“아니 라벨은 있는데 왜 못 읽냐고!! 내가 너무 잘 보호했어...”
