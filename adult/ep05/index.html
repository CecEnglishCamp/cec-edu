📞 Making a Phone Call

전화하기 — Phone English

⭐ Key Expressions

✓Paper or plastic?
종이봉투요, 비닐봉투요? (마트 계산대 표현)

📖 Story Preview

Young-ja goes out with her daughter. They go to the store and a café. In the store, the cart is hard to push. Young-ja says, "This cart hates me." At checkout, the worker says, "Paper or plastic?" Young-ja doesn't understand. Her daughter says, "It means shopping bag!" She orders iced Americano at the café. She drinks the coffee. It tastes very strong. She says, "This is not coffee… this is medicine!"...

"장바구니 말하는 거예요, 엄마."

"아이스 아메리카노! 라지! 투 고!"

"…이건 물도 아니고, 커피도 아니고, 그냥 밤샘용 액체야…"
