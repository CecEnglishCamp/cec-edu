⭐ Key Expressions

✓I'd like to make an appointment.

📖 Story Preview

Young-ja feels sick. She calls the hospital. “Hello! I want… appointment!” The person asks, “Why are you sick?” Young-ja says, “My nose is angry. My throat is spicy!” They say, “Please wait.” After some time, a nurse talks. Young-ja says, “My head is balloon. But I’m not die. Just a little.” They say, “Come at 2 p.m.” She smiles. “I did it!”...

“헬로! 아이 원 투 메이크 어… 어… 어펄먼트!”

“Okay, what’s the reason for your visit?”

“My nose is... angry! And my throat is very… spicy!”

“Please hold while we connect you to a nurse.”
