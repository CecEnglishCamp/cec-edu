⭐ Key Expressions

✓What did you bring?

📖 Story Preview

Young-ja goes to church. It is potluck day. She brings Korean noodles. One man says, “Spicy?” She says, “No, not spicy!” People eat the noodles. One person puts ketchup on them! A kid eats them like cake and cries. Young-ja says, “Next time, I bring kimbap!”...

💬 Dialog

“엄마, 이번 주 교회 Potluck이에요. 음식 하나만 가져오면 돼요!”

“오! Is that Korean noodles? Spicy?”

“No no... it’s not spicy. Just... little soy sauce with... sesame!”

“맛있다~ 케첩이랑 잘 어울리네요!”

“케첩?! 잡채에 케첩은 아니지!!”

“다음엔 불고기 김밥으로 간다. 절대 헷갈릴 수 없게.”
