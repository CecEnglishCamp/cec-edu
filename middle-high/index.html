Camp B

Select Your School ↓

Essay writing, structured debate, and critical thinking — built for students ready to argue and write confidently in English at secondary school level and beyond.

📝
Essay Writing Workshop

Write a structured essay and get line-by-line AI feedback covering grammar, logic, and expression at your exact grade level.

⚔️
Debate Training

Year 2 argues pros & cons; Year 3 runs full three-round debates with opening, rebuttal, and closing.

💬
Discussion & Wrap-Up

Every session closes with guided discussion and a ChatGPT link for open-ended conversation practice.

📊
Grade-Differentiated Feedback

AI feedback calibrated per grade — middle school gets accessible corrections; high school receives college-level critique.
