📌 Key Sentences

✓Red Riding Hood went to the bed.

침대로 갔어요.

💡 went to = ~로 갔다

✓What big eyes you have Grandma!

할머니 눈이 정말 크시네요!

💡 What big = 감탄문

✓The better to see you!

너를 더 잘 보려고!

💡 The better to = 더 잘 ~하려고

✓What big ears you have!

귀가 정말 크시네요!

💡 big ears = 큰 귀

✓The better to hear you!

너를 더 잘 들으려고!

💡 hear = 듣다
