📌 Key Sentences

✓A big wolf appeared in the forest!

큰 늑대가 숲에 나타났어요!

💡 appeared = 나타났다

✓Where are you going?

어디 가는 거야?

💡 Where = 어디

✓I am going to Grandma's house.

할머니 집에 가요.

💡 going to = ~에 가는 중

✓The wolf smiled.

늑대가 웃었어요.

💡 smiled = 미소 지었다

✓Red Riding Hood was not scared.

빨간 모자는 무서워하지 않았어요.

💡 was not scared = 무섭지 않았다
